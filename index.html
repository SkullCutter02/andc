about us

Led by students who are passionate about art, ANDF is a service aimed at not only promoting art within the CIS community and beyond, but also teaching people practical skills in hopes that they will be able to apply their knowledge of art and design within any future endeavours.

In our service, courses are blends of traditional and modern forms of art and design, which includes sketching and oil painting as well as activities involving apps like Procreate and Blender. Volunteers will mainly teach art courses to primary students and course material will be inspected by student leaders beforehand.
